DESCRIPTION

The SEEWORLD R58L is an easy-to-install OBD2 GPS tracking device. By connecting this OBD GPS tracker to your car’s OBD-II port, you can avoid time-consuming installation; there’s also no need to worry about unexpected power shortages, and it delivers 24 hours of continuous GPS tracking and monitoring for your vehicle.


You get the driving route, stay length, driving speed and direction, vehicle diagnostics, and other useful OBD information that regular OBD GPS trackers do not get. The Geo-Fence feature allows you to restrict vehicle movement inside a district and receive notifications if it leaves it.
